# Employer Brand Scoring Scale

The workflow set needs one shared scoring language so different analysts do not encode the same finding in incompatible ways.

## Message Presence Scale

- `Strong` - a primary message with repeated proof across important surfaces
- `Present` - clearly visible but not central
- `Weak` - lightly signaled or implied
- `Absent` - not found in reviewed evidence
- `Unknown` - cannot be assessed from available evidence

## Confidence Scale

- `High` - multiple direct proof points from important surfaces
- `Medium` - at least one direct proof point, limited breadth, or mild ambiguity
- `Low` - inference-heavy or weakly supported

## Freshness Flags

- `Current`
- `Aging`
- `Stale`

## Usage Rules

- Do not collapse presence and confidence into one value.
- Use `Unknown` when evidence is missing, not `Absent`.
- Numeric scoring is optional and should not be introduced unless the team agrees on calibration rules.
