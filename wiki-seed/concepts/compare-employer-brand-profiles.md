# Compare Employer Brand Profiles

This process produces a [Employer Brand Comparison](../entities/employer-brand-comparison.md) from at least two profiles.

## Inputs

- Two or more company profiles
- Agreed scoring vocabulary
- Shared scope assumptions

## Outputs

- Cross-company matrix
- Shared-theme summary
- Differentiation summary
- Whitespace and risk calls

## Recommended Steps

1. Confirm that the profiles are comparable in scope.
2. Align on the message dimensions being scored.
3. Compare each company by presence, proof, tone, and channel emphasis.
4. Summarize the shared conventions in the category.
5. Identify where the client can credibly differentiate.
6. Call out the evidence gaps that weaken the readout.

## Rules

- Comparison is an interpretation layer, not a data dump.
- Do not score dimensions the evidence cannot support.
- Preserve local or audience-specific nuance where it materially changes the story.
