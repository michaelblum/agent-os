# Collect Employer Brand Profile Artifacts

This process converts an intake brief into a capture plan and a usable evidence bundle.

## Inputs

- Intake brief
- Company set
- Target audience and market
- Agreed surfaces and constraints

## Outputs

- Collection manifest
- Raw artifact bundle
- Gap log

## Recommended Steps

1. Confirm scope before collecting anything.
2. Enumerate capture surfaces by company.
3. Write stable request identifiers and priorities.
4. Specify what evidence matters for each request.
5. Collect the artifacts.
6. Log blocked, missing, or low-quality captures.

## Surface Priorities

- Careers site and EVP pages
- Role-specific landing pages
- Social employer-brand channels
- Employee-review platforms
- Awards, rankings, and reputation proof
- Local or market-specific recruitment pages

## Collection Rules

- Plan first, collect second.
- Keep company coverage reasonably symmetric for comparison work.
- Capture enough context around the claim, not just the quote itself.
- Preserve failed or blocked requests as visible gaps.
