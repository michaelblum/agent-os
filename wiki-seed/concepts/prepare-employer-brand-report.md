# Prepare Employer Brand Report

Report prep is the packaging stage. It should not introduce new analysis.

## Inputs

- Employer Brand Comparison
- Intended audience
- Delivery format and constraints

## Outputs

- Report outline
- Narrative summary
- Asset checklist
- Optional structured report payload

## Recommended Steps

1. Choose the audience and decision to support.
2. Pull the minimum evidence needed to defend the claims.
3. Sequence the narrative from context to comparison to action.
4. Package evidence visuals and citations for the chosen format.
5. Highlight unknowns that could change the recommendation.

## Delivery Modes

- Executive readout
- Analyst appendix
- Portable report package
- Presentation brief for a separate design layer
